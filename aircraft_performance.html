Defaults are Conklin & de Decker baseline figures embedded at build time. Fuel scales with burn (gal/hr × price) and maintenance labor with man-hours per flight hour × rate, so edited prices propagate physically rather than proportionally. Charter offset assumes the owner bears variable cost on charter hours and receives the rate less management commission; the breakeven rate is the charter price at which an additional charter hour neither adds nor removes annual cost. Internal planning tool — not a quote.
